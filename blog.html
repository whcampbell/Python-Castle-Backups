IVy's Journal

I don't like the urge for a publicized life. I understand that true validation cannot come from an audience, but must be found within. In short, I feel a little embarrassed writing a blog.

However, I enjoy writing. It seems to slow down the firehose of thoughts that cascades through my mind at all times. I've also been told that I'm a good writer, and people enjoy what I've created.

To that end, I hope you enjoy something you find here. Even better, I wish you love and happiness.
