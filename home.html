I guess I could say that this website exists for a few reasons. The first and foremost was for me to get a job.

Not that switching on a Django website right out of the box is all that impressive. I just wanted to make it clear that I know at least a thing or two. My games can also speak for that (hopefully).

The last reason was that I wanted attention. Not really, I explain a little further in the Journal pages. That being said, a bit of attention would not hurt.

No matter what reason you visited, I hope that you enjoy what you find.
Thanks for being here.
